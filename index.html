About your Tutor

Pete is a practicing and fully qualified maths teacher working from the sunny Yorkshire Coast. He’s taught young people of all ages for the past 18 years and love’s his subject.

Whilst many people find maths either too difficult or dull, Pete creates an informal, friendly stress-free environment for his pupils, enabling them to overcome a lack of confidence, dramatically improving their grades.

Up-to-date with latest exam board approaches, all Pete’s lessons are meticulously planned and tie in with current class room work. He holds an Enhanced DBS certificate and can provide his certificate number on request.
