About your Tutor

Hi, I'm Pete Finch and I've been teaching for the past 18 years, both in the classroom and ona one-to-one basis.

I love my subject and am aware that many people find maths either too difficult or dull. So with that in mind, I always try and create an informal, friendly stress-free environment for my pupils, enabling them to overcome a lack of confidence and often dramatically improving their grades.

Up-to-date with all the latest exam board approaches, my lessons are meticulously planned and usually tie in with current class room work. I hold a valid Enhanced DBS certificate and can provide my certificate number on request.
